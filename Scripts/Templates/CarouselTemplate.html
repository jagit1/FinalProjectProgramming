Climate change is the major political and scientific challenge of our time. The need to reduce greenhouse gas emissions globally in line with the Paris Agreement is widely recognised. It is also known that ease of access to data on various environmental indicators is vital to secure public buy in to climate change mitigation measures.


The indicators show trends in key environmental quality indicators globally and in the Irish context. See and compare trends in indicators like greenhouse gas emissions across countries, levels of electricity production using renewable fuel sources globally, energy consumption trends across time by different economic sectors in the Irish context as well as a number of others.
